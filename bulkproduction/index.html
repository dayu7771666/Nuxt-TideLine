A Systematic Approach to Swimwear Excellence

From Concept to Commercial Reality

Once Partner with Tideline, you can wit the collaborative, detail-oriented journey your designs undertake. This transparent process, built on clear communication and precision execution at every stage(from concept to commercial reality), is designed to ensure your vision is perfectly realized in full-scale production.

The Pillars of Excellence: Core Stages in Tideline Manufacturing Process

Design & Development

Our design process merges aesthetic trends with performance data, creating styles tested for >90% customer satisfaction on fit. We leverage 3D design prototyping to accelerate development, reducing sampling cycles by 30% and ensuring styles are both commercially viable and functionally superior.

Pattern Making & Prototyping

We utilize digital precision patterning to engineer flawless fit from the start. Each prototype undergoes a 5-point fit test on various body types, with adjustments made to key areas like the bust, waist, and hips. This rigorous process ensures a 98% size consistency rate across all production batches.

Fabric Sourcing & Sustainability

Over 80% of our fabrics sourced from leading eco-conscious mills. All materials offer UPF protection and are lab-tested to withstand UV and chlorine exposure without significant degradation.

Manufacturing & Quality Assurance

From stitch density to elastic tension, each detail is measured against stringent standards. This results in a product defect rate of less than 0.5%, ensuring that your brand receives bulk orders that are consistently high-quality and market-ready.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We believe beautiful swimwear shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. That's why sustainability is woven into every aspect of our production process.

Eco-Friendly Materials

We prioritize fabrics made from recycled materials and sustainable sources. Our packaging is 100% recyclable, and we're continuously exploring innovative materials.

Ethical Production

We partner with manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor practices. Our artisans work in safe environments and receive fair wages.

Waste Reduction

Through efficient pattern cutting and production planning, we minimize textile waste. Any leftover materials are repurposed or recycled whenever possible.
