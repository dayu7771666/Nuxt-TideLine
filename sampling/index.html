How Our Sampling Process Works

Our streamlined 6-step process ensures you get maximum value from your swimwear sampling project with clear deliverables at each stage.

1

Design & Fit Strategy Review

We analyze your tech pack and designs to establish the foundational fit block, target market measurements, and discuss key swim-specific construction techniques (e.g., flatlock stitching, lining, elastic application).

2

Fabric & Trim Curation

We present and source approved swim-specific fabrics (e.g., nylon-spandex blends, recycled materials) and critical trims (e.g., chlorine-resistant elastics, bust cups, hardware) based on your performance and aesthetic needs.

3

Pattern Engineering & Mock-up

Our technicians draft the first precision swimwear pattern, accounting for fabric stretch and recovery. A preliminary muslin or digital mock-up is often created to validate the fit on a form before cutting the final fabric.

4

First Article Assembly & Internal Fitting

The first physical sample is constructed. We conduct a rigorous internal fit session on a live model or mannequin, checking for garment tension, gapping, and mobility to identify any construction adjustments.

5

Sample Finishing & Quality Gate

The sample undergoes final finishing (e.g., hook & eye application, label sewing) and a stringent quality inspection for stitching integrity, seam flatness, and trim placement against your specifications.

6

Feedback Integration & Production Pathway

You receive the sample for evaluation. We consolidate your feedback on fit, fabric hand-feel, and overall aesthetic to refine the prototype and present a clear roadmap to production, including grading rules for your size set.
